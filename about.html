About Pharmacographics

Pharmacographics is an online drug information resource that provides easy to understand illustrations, graphics, and language to educate people interested in learning more about their medications.

  • Pharmacographics is a publication and community project of pharmacyinformatics.net
  • Pharmacographics is always accepting content, so feel free to contact us
